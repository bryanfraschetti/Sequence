Sequence

Get the most out of Spotify's unique crossfading feature and rearrange your playlists to flow based on key signature and tempo

How it Works

When you click "Get Started" you will be redirected to spotify, which will ask you to grant this app some permissions regarding your spotify account. These are explained on the about section. After accepting the permissions, simply load your playlist, select the sequencing method, pick the first song, and a new playlist will be created.
